Arcadia Bay

A closer look at the town.

Introduction

Arcadia Bay is a picturesque seaside town in Oregon, United States. The town is experiencing strange weather and other phenomena that is somehow linked to Max Caulfield's time-controlling abilities.

Arcadia Bay has a large and diverse range of wildlife. It varies from different types of birds to the various deer that you see throughout the episodes of the game. However, not much is said regarding the different types of animal species in Arcadia Bay.

Key Locations

Blackwell Academy

Blackwell Academy is a senior high school which specializes in Science and Arts, and is currently led by Ray Wells.

According to Ms. Grant, the school is built on old Native American land, shared between the natives and the early colonial settlers.

Blackwell is partially owned by, and financially supported by, the Prescott family. In 1998, they donated the dormitory building to Blackwell.

Two Whales Diner

The Two Whales Diner is a local diner in Arcadia Bay. Chloe's mother, Joyce, works there as a waitress.

It is clear that the diner is hugely popular, particularly with the students in the area. Police officers, passing truck drivers and other residents of Arcadia Bay also frequent the diner, possibly due to its close proximity to a bus stop, a large parking space next to and opposite the diner and a gas station nearby.

Lighthouse

The Lighthouse is located in the forested outskirts of Arcadia Bay. For locals who want to go to the lighthouse they must follow an uphill pathway in the forest which ends up by the ocean cliff.

Max tells us that she and Chloe used to spend a lot of time there when they were young. Visitors and guests at the lighthouse are encouraged to leave the wildlife in the area alone, as per the sign on the trail. Max confirms it's been there a long time.

Arcadia Bay
A Brief History

The history of Arcadia Bay's beginnings is relatively obscured. The oldest family that lived in the town is the Prescott Family, who've worked to build or were involved in major aspects of Arcadia's Bay economy and education. Speaking with the fisherman at Two Whales Diner, he confirms that one of Arcadia Bay's primary exports is fishing.


However, when the Prescott family gained more influence over the town, they used their finances to limit fishing by gaining rights to the harbor. Their contributions to the town are such that there is legitimate consideration to change the name from "Arcadia Bay" to "Prescott Bay".

Other Important Locations

Junkyard

American Rust is Arcadia Bay's junkyard, considered as a second home by Chloe. She and Rachel Amber spent a lot of time there, evident from their hideout in a wrecked outhouse there. The hideout is covered in their belongings and graffiti. The junkyard is located next to the train tracks.

The train that passes by in episode two has the number 1337. It later appears derailed on the Two Whales Diner street in episode five, as a consequence of the storm.

Madsen Household

The Madsen Household is where David Madsen, Joyce Madsen and her daughter Chloe Price live. It is the previous residence of William Price before his untimely death. Max revisits the house when she is reunited with Chloe.

The house has two bedrooms, including Chloe's room and Joyce and David's room, one bathroom, an open plan kitchen-living room, a back yard and a garage, mostly used by David.

Dark Room

The Dark Room is a secret bunker, hidden under the Prescott Barn and used by Mark Jefferson for his illegal photographing of girls. It is filled with survival supplies and expensive photography equipment.

Tortured guro images adorn the walls in the bunker; presumably put up by either Nathan Prescott or his father Sean Prescott, as the style is more similar to Nathan's than that of Mr. Jefferson. Needles sit on one of the side tables, obviously used for sedating the victims or those involved.

Prescott Barn

The Prescott Barn is owned by the Prescott family, officially under the name of Harry Aaron Prescott. It is an old, dilapidated and explorable area in Episode Four: "Dark Room". Max and Chloe are led to the barn after their investigation into Nathan's involvement in drugging Kate Marsh.

Max finds a trapdoor, which is suspiciously more modern than the rest of the barn. She breaks the lock, and the girls discover a code-locked door leading to what is known as the Dark Room, or the Prescott Bunker.

Beach

The Beach is a location first seen in episode two. Previous to that, we can catch a glimpse of it by looking towards the sea at the lighthouse.

Trying to find out more about Rachel Amber's disappearance, Max and Chloe drive to the beach to confront Frank and get a list of his clients. This results in a complex showdown that ends with Frank getting possibly shot or stabbed, though it is possible to befriend him.

Hospital

The Hospital is located in Arcadia Bay and, depending on the player's choices, can first be seen in episode two. There is another determinant chance to visit the hospital again in episode four.

If the player manages to stop Kate Marsh from committing suicide, the hospital can first be seen in the following ending cutscene, as Kate is depicted lying in a bed with several balloons tied to it.
