Max Caulfield

Maxine Caulfield, better known as Max (born September 21, 1995).

"With great power comes great bullshit."

She is an aspiring photographer and senior at Blackwell Academy. She left her mom and dad in Seattle for the seaside town of Arcadia Bay, Oregon, where she grew up.

After saving her childhood friend Chloe Price from being killed by Nathan Prescott in one of Blackwell Academy's bathrooms, she discovers she has the ability to rewind time - and even stop it in its tracks.

On the search for clues to solve the mysterious disappearance of Rachel Amber, Max must also discover how to use her powers to save the town of Arcadia Bay from a tornado.

Personality and Interests

Max has her own dorm room at Blackwell Academy.
"If I'm not looking through a viewfinder, I'm looking through a window... Always looking."

Max loves eating at Two Whales Diner.
"The only thing that's changed on the menu are the prices..."

Max enjoys playing guitar and listening to music.
"It's such a pretty guitar. I need to play it more."

Max always has her camera with her and regularly takes photos.
"I take photos. Of me, the world, everything. It may sound sad, but I have a blast."
