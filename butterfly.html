The Butterfly Effect


Time Travel

Max's ability to travel through time allows her to alter anything she wants. This means that she can constantly alter the course of both her past and future. This can have fatal effects on both herself and others.

Alternate Universe

As a result of saving Chloe's father William, Max is returned to an alternate Universe where William is alive. However, in this universe Chloe is paralysed and terminally ill, leaving Max with some big decisions to make.

Max's Choices and Actions

Every choice made by Max has an effect on the future. The most subtle of interactions can prove to have long lasting damage. When Max decides to make fun of Victoria as this leads to Victoria not trusting Max.

Key Choices

Episode 1 - Chrysalis

Decisions that had most impact on the future of Max.

  • Choice 1

    Tell on Nathan or hide the truth

    Max catches Nathan in the girls bathroom with a gun. When confronted by Principal Wells, Max can either tell on Nathan which will put her in danger or hide the truth which will keep Nathan off her back, but could keep others at danger.

  • Choice 2

    Make fun of or comfort Victoria

    A bucket of paint is dropped onto Victoria as she was blocking Max from entering the dorms. This leaves Victoria red-faced and Max can either comfort her, which will help Max gain Victoria's trust, or she can make fun of her. This will make their relationship even worse.

  • Choice 3

    Take a photo or intervene when David hassles Kate

    Max notices David harrassing Kate and has to choose whether to take a photo and use it as evidence, or intervene and help Kate. If you decide to take a photo Kate will become annoyed, while she will be thankful and trust you more if you intervene.

  • Choice 4

    Take the blame for Chloe or hide

    David returns home and questions Chloe about the weed he finds in her room. Max is hidden in the closet and must decide to take the blame or stay hidden. If you take the blame, David will grow suspicious of you and will act aggressive towards you. If you hide, Chloe will feel let down by you.

  • End of

Key Choices

Episode 2 - Out of Time

  • Choice 1

    Tell Kate to go to the police or look for proof

    When Kate tells Max that she thinks she was drugged by Nathan Prescott at the Vortex Club party, Max must decide to tell the police or look for proof. If you decide to tell the police, Kate will try to include you as a witness which Max doesn't like. If you tell Kate to look for proof, she becomes annoyed and questions your trust.

  • Choice 2

    Answer or don't answer Kate's call

    Kate calls Max as she is about to leave the diner with Chloe. If Max answers, Chloe feels offended as she believes Max cares more about Kate than her. If Max doesn't answer, Kate becomes bitter towards Max and believes that she is not a real friend.

  • Choice 3

    Shoot or don't shoot Frank

    Frank confronts Chloe and Max about the money Chloe owes him. Max pulls a gun on Frank and he dares her to shoot him. If Max decides to shoot, the gun doesn't fire as it isn't loaded. This makes Frank angry and puts Max in danger. If Max doesn't shoot, Frank takes the gun and Chloe feels disappointed in Max.

  • Choice 4

    Saving or not saving Kate's Life

    Kate is about to commit suicide on the roof of the dorms. Max reaches her and pleads with her to come down. If you have been nice to Kate and helped her when she needed you, then you can convince her to come down. However, if you have not been a good friend to her she will jump and kill herself.

  • Choice 5

    Choosing who to blame for Kate's problem

    Max is taken to the principal's office to tell him what led Kate to the rooftop. Max can choose to blame David, Nathan or Mr. Jefferson. If she blames David, he may bring up the fact that she was caught with weed. If she blames Nathan, he will say it was Kate's fault and if she blames Jefferson, he will be removed from hosting the Everyday Heroes content in San Francisco.

  • End of

Key Choices

Episode 3 - Chaos Theory

  • Choice 1

    Steal or leave the money

    Max and Chloe find a bag with $5,000 in it. It is supposed to go towards the handicapped fund, but Chloe wants to take it to payback Frank. If you steal the money Chloe can use it to bargain with Frank when getting information about Rachel. If you leave it, Frank will be difficult to persuade.

  • Choice 2

    Kiss or don't kiss Chloe

    Chloe dares Max to kiss her in her bedroom. If Max kisses her, Chloe will feel a much closer bond to Max and will be given the option to kiss her again later in the game. If Max doesn't kiss Chloe, Chloe will claim that Max probably kissed her and rewinded time.

  • Choice 3

    Side with David or side with Chloe

    David and Chloe argue about the surveillance camera's in the house. If you side with David, Chloe will become upset with Max and ask you to rewind the situation. If you side with Chloe David will eventually be kicked out of the house and will not speak to Max again.

  • Choice 4

    Harm or don't harm Pompidou

    Frank's dog Pompidou runs towards you when you enter his RV. If you throw the bone towards the road, Pompidou will be hit by a truck and eventually die resulting in Max feeling extremely guilty. If you throw the bone into the parking lot, Pompidou will not be hurt and Frank will not get angry.

  • Choice 5

    Take or leave David's gun

    If Max did not attempt to shoot Frank, she will have the option to steal a gun from David to give to Chloe. If Max takes the gun, Frank can be killed in the meeting at the beach. If Max leaves the gun it is more difficult to kill Frank as Chloe must take a gun from Nathan if he is beaten up by Warren.

  • End of
    Episode

Key Choices

Episode 4 - Dark Room

  • Choice 1

    Accept or refuse Chloe's request

    Max finds herself in an alternate universe where Chloe is seriously ill. Chloe asks Max to end her life and leaves Max with a heart-breaking decision. If Max accepts, Chloe will congratulate her for following her dreams and die. If she refuses, Chloe will get angry and ask her to leave, giving Max a chance to return to her real reality.

  • Choice 2

    Stay out of it or stop Warren

    Nathan confronts Max and Chloe when he finds them in the boys dorm. When Nathan takes out a gun Warren jumps on him and starts beating him up. If Max decides to stay out of it, Warren will continue to hit Nathan until he drops his gun, which Chloe picks up. If Max stops Warren, Nathan will not drop his gun.

  • Choice 3

    Kill, harm or don't harm Frank

    Max and chloe go to see Frank to ask him about Rachel. As the coversation progresses Max and Chloe can end up killing, harming or not harming Frank. If they kill Frank, Chloe will take the code from Frank before leaving. If they harm Frank, Chloe will take the code and the gun from Frank. If they do not harm Frank, he will trust them and give them the code and maybe David's gun.

  • Choice 4

    Warn or don't warn Victoria

    At the Vortex club party, Max runs into Victoria and has the chance to warn her about the Dark room. If Victoria believes Max's warning, she will be drugged and killed. If she does not believe Max's warning she will not be drugged or killed. If Max doesn't warn her, Victoria will not be drugged or killed.

  • End of
    Episode
    4

Key Choices

Episode 5 - Polarized

  • Choice 1

    David kills or doesn't kill Jefferson

    When David free's you from the Dark room, he asks you about Chloe. If you tell him that Chloe is dead, David will become enraged and shoot Jefferson who is lying handcuffed and unconcious on the floor. If you hide the truth, David will feel relieved and will not kill Jefferson.

  • Choice 2

    Help or don't help Joyce with David

    Max meets Joyce at the diner and talks to her about David. If Max tells Joyce that David saved her life, Joyce will take him back to the house. If Max convinces Joyce to not take David back, she will not let him back into the house, claiming that he sometimes scares her.

  • Choice 3

    Kiss, hug or ignore Warren

    When Max and Warren meet in the diner, Max has the chance to show affection towards Warren. If Max kisses him, Warren will try to confess his feelings but Max will interrupt him. If Max hugs Warren, he will appear in her nightmare complaining that she didn't kiss him. If Max does not show affection, she will ask him to take care of Joyce before focusing on the photo.

  • Choice 4

    Sacrifice Chloe or Arcadia Bay

    The final choice of the game forces Max to choose between going back in time to let Chloe die, thus saving Arcadia Bay. Or tearing up the butterfly polaroid and destroying the town. If you sacrifice Chloe, Max will travel back to the bathroom and let Nathan shoot her, this will prompt a funeral cutscene which ends the game. If you save Chloe, Max and Chloe will leave Arcadia Bay in ruins and drive away together.

  • End of
    Episode
